About

Since our inception in 2017, Kev Guru Media has focused on delivering high-quality web development and social media management services. We have had the privilege of working with industry giants such as Fetty Wap, PnB Rock, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, NBA Youngboy, Burna Boy, and many others. At Kev Guru Media, we specialize in creating sleek, user-friendly websites and applications while helping businesses grow through strategic social media management. We combine innovation and creativity to help our clients stand out in the digital world.
