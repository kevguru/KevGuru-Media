About

Since our inception in 2017, Kev Guru Media has embarked on an inspiring creative journey, exploring the dynamic realms of social media, web development, digital content creation, and music production. Our work has graced projects for industry giants such as Fetty Wap, PnB Rock, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, Burna Boy, Holy Gxd, Lil Skies, RGF Productions, Atlantic Records, and more. At Kev Guru Media, we blend artistic prowess with technological innovation to produce captivating animations, engaging visual content, groundbreaking media experiences, and innovative music. Welcome to our world.
