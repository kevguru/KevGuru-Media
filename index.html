About

Since our inception in 2017, Kev Guru Media has embarked on an inspiring creative journey, exploring the dynamic realms of social media, web development, digital content creation, and music production. Our work has graced projects for industry giants such as Fetty Wap, PnB Rock, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, NBA Youngboy, Burna Boy, Holy Gxd, Lil Skies, RGF Productions, Atlantic Records, American Music Group, Guwii Kidz, Tapia SGB, Rgf M80 and more. At Kev Guru Media, we blend artistic prowess with technological innovation to produce captivating animations, engaging visual content, groundbreaking media experiences, and innovative music.

Our Services

  • Web and App Development: Crafting sleek, user-friendly websites and applications.
  • Animations: Breathing life into stories with dynamic and visually stunning animations.
  • Graphic Design: Designing eye-catching visuals that communicate your brand’s message.
  • Logos: Creating impactful and memorable brand identities.
  • Songwriting: Crafting lyrics and melodies that resonate.
  • Mixing and Mastering: Polishing your music to perfection for a professional sound.
  • Music Video Creation: Producing cinematic visuals that amplify your music’s impact.
  • Social Media Management: Building and nurturing your online presence to drive engagement and growth.
